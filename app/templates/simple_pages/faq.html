What is the goal of the game?

Your goal is to get your passive income (also called "Cashflow") higher than your expenses. You might think that the best paying profession is the most liekly to win the game but remember one thing: the higher your income, the more likely it is that you spend more. You may have a high income, but you also need more Cashflow to work against your expenses.
